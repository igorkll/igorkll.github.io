Commands:

  • 1. /tpa (nickname) - send a request to teleport you to a player. They can accept using /accept
  • 2. /tpra (nickname) - send a request to the player to teleport them to you. They can accept using /accept
  • 3. /accept (id(default: 1)) - accept any action
  • 4. /decline (id(default: 1)) - decline any action
  • 5. /online - displays the list of players in the game in the local chat
  • 6. /pos - displays your position in the chat
  • 7. /rtp - teleports to a random point on the map
  • 8. /reg (password) (accept password) - sets the password on your nickname
  • 9. /passwd (old password) (new password) (accept password) - changes the password on your nickname
  • 10. /login (password) - used to enter the password for your nickname when logging onto the server

Nickname Requirements:

  • 1. Maximum length of 20 characters
  • 2. Do not use spaces or special characters
  • 3. Only small and capital Latin letters, numbers, and symbols: _ - . are allowed
